I recently graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras. Currently, I am a research assistant at AI4Bharat, where I work under the guidance of Prof. Mitesh M. Khapra.

My primary research interests lie in Text-to-Speech (TTS) technology, Computational Linguistics, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Machine Learning. A major focus of my work is on developing models and datasets for under-resourced languages to make NLP technology more widely accessible. As part of this effort, I have worked on Indic-TextNorm and Indic-Num2Words.

Previously, I studied Product Design and Manufacture at the University of Nottingham, UK, where I worked on several design projects under the supervision of Prof. Richard Tew.

If you'd like to discuss research, academia, or anything else, feel free to reach out at 21f1006390@ds.study.iitm.ac.in
